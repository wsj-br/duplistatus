USE, REPRODUCTION, AUR DISTRIBUTION KE LIYE SHARTEN AUR SHARTEN​

1. Paribhashayen​

"License" ka matlab is dastavez ke Sections 1 se 9 dwara paribhashit istemal, punarutpadan, aur vitran ke liye sharten aur sharten hongi.

"Licensor" ka matlab copyright malik ya copyright malik dwara pradhikrit ikai hogi jo License pradan kar raha hai.

"Legal Entity" ka matlab acting entity aur anya sabhi entities ka sangam hoga jo us entity ko niyantrit karti hain, niyantrit hain, ya uske saath saamanya niyantran mein hain. Is paribhasha ke uddeshyon ke liye, "niyantran" ka matlab hai (i) contract dwara ya anyatha, aisi entity ke disha ya prabandhan ko karne ki shakti, seedhi ya paroksh, ya (ii) outstanding shares ka pachas pratishat (50%) ya usse adhik ka swaamitva, ya (iii) aisi entity ka labhkari swaamitva.

"Aap" (ya "Aapka") ka matlab ek vyakti ya Legal Entity hogi jo is License dwara pradan ki gayi anumatiyon ka upyog kar raha hai.

"Source" roop ka matlab sanshodhan karne ka pasandida roop hoga, jisme software source code, dastavej source, aur configuration file shamil hain, lekin yah tak seemit nahin hai.

"Object" roop ka matlab Source roop ke yantrik roopantaran ya anuvaad se utpann koi bhi roop hoga, jisme compiled object code, utpann dastavej, aur anya media types mein roopantaran shamil hain, lekin yah tak seemit nahin hai.

"Work" ka matlab rachna ka karya hoga, chahe Source ya Object roop mein, jo License ke tahat uplabdh karaya gaya hai, jaisa ki karya mein shamil ya usse juda copyright suchna dwara sanket kiya gaya hai (ek udaharan anushuchi mein pradan kiya gaya hai).

"Derivative Works" ka matlab koi bhi karya hoga, chahe Source ya Object roop mein, jo Work par aadharit hai (ya usse utpann hua hai) aur jiske liye sampadkiya sanshodhan, tippaniyan, vistar, ya anya sanshodhan samuh roop se, rachna ka ek mool karya prastut karte hain. Is License ke uddeshyon ke liye, Derivative Works mein aise karya shamil nahin honge jo Work aur uske Derivative Works se alag rahate hain, ya keval link (ya naam se bandhe) karte hain.

"Contribution" ka matlab rachna ka koi bhi karya hoga, jisme Work ka mool sanskaran aur us Work ya uske Derivative Works mein sanshodhan ya vriddhi shamil hai, jo copyright malik dwara ya copyright malik ki taraf se submit karne ke liye pradhikrit vyakti ya Legal Entity dwara Work mein shamil karne ke liye Licensor ko swachha roop se submit kiya gaya hai. Is paribhasha ke uddeshyon ke liye, "submit kiya gaya" ka matlab Licensor ya uske pratinidhiyon ko bheji gayi electronic, maukhik, ya likhit sanchar ka koi bhi roop hoga, jisme electronic mailing list, source code control system, aur issue tracking system mein sanchar shamil hai jo Work par charcha aur sudhar ke uddeshya se Licensor dwara ya uski taraf se prabandhit kiye jate hain, lekin aise sanchar ko chhodkar jo spasht roop se "Not a Contribution" ke roop mein chinhit kiya gaya hai ya anyatha likhit roop mein designate kiya gaya hai.

"Contributor" ka matlab Licensor aur koi bhi vyakti ya Legal Entity hogi jiske taraf se Licensor dwara ek Contribution prapt hui hai aur baad mein Work mein shamil ki gayi hai.

2. Copyright License ka Anudan​

Is License ki sharton aur sharton ke adheen, pratyek Contributor is dwara aapko Work aur aise Derivative Works ko Source ya Object roop mein punarutpadit karne, Derivative Works taiyar karne, sarvajanik roop se pradarshit karne, sarvajanik roop se pradarshan karne, sublicense karne, aur vitrit karne ke liye ek sthayi, vishwa-vyapi, gair-ekmatra, bina-shulk, royalty-mukt, atal copyright license pradan karta hai.

3. Patent License ka Anudan​

Is License ki sharton aur niyam-o-sharton ke adheen, har Contributor is prakaar aapko ek sthaayi, vishwa-vyapi, gair-vishesh, bina kisi shulk ke, royalty-mukt, atal (jab tak ki is section mein bataya gaya ho) patent license pradaan karta hai ki aap Work ko bana, banva, istemaal, bechne ki peshkash, bech, aayat, aur anyatha hastantarit kar sakein, jahaan aisi license kewal un patent dharon par lagu hoti hai jinhe aise Contributor dwara license diya ja sakta hai jo anivarya roop se unke Contribution(s) dwara akele ya unke Contribution(s) ke Work ke saath sanyojan dwara ullanghan karte hain jismein aise Contribution(s) jama kiye gaye the. Yadi aap kisi entity ke khilaaf patent litigation shuru karte hain (kisi mukadme mein cross-claim ya counterclaim sahit) yeh dava karte hue ki Work ya Work mein shaamil ek Contribution seedhe ya contributory patent ullanghan karta hai, to us Work ke liye is License ke tahat aapko pradaan ki gayi koi bhi patent license litigation daayir kiye jaane ki taareekh se samapt ho jayegi.

4. Punah Vitran​

Aap kisi bhi madhyam mein Work ya uske Derivative Works ki pratiyon ko, sanshodhan ke saath ya bina, aur Source ya Object roop mein, punah utpadit aur vitrit kar sakte hain, yadi aap nimnalikhit sharten poori karte hain:

(a) Aapko Work ya Derivative Works ke kisi bhi anya praaptakarta ko is License ki ek prati deni hogi; aur

(b) Aapko kisi bhi sanshodhit file mein yeh spasht suchna deni hogi ki aapne files mein badlav kiya hai; aur

(c) Aapko, aap dwara vitrit ki jaane wali kisi bhi Derivative Works ke Source roop mein, Work ke Source roop se sabhi copyright, patent, trademark, aur shrey-naam suchnaon ko barkarar rakhna hoga, un suchnaon ko chhodkar jo Derivative Works ke kisi bhi hisse se sambandhit nahi hain; aur

(d) Yadi Work mein uske vitran ke bhaag ke roop mein "NOTICE" text file shaamil hai, to aap dwara vitrit ki jaane wali kisi bhi Derivative Works mein aise NOTICE file mein shaamil shrey-naam suchnaon ki ek pathneey prati shaamil honi chahiye, un suchnaon ko chhodkar jo Derivative Works ke kisi bhi hisse se sambandhit nahi hain, nimnalikhit mein se kam se kam ek sthan par: Derivative Works ke bhaag ke roop mein vitrit ki jaane wali ek NOTICE text file mein; Source roop ya dastavezikaran mein, yadi Derivative Works ke saath pradaan kiya gaya ho; ya, Derivative Works dwara utpann ek display mein, yadi aur jahaan bhi aisi teesri-party suchnaayein aam taur par dikhai deti hain. NOTICE file ki samagri kewal soochanaatmak uddeshyon ke liye hai aur License ko sanshodhit nahi karti hai. Aap apni alag shrey-naam suchnaayein un Derivative Works mein jod sakte hain jinhe aap vitrit karte hain, Work se NOTICE text ke saath ya uske alawa, yadi aisi atirikt shrey-naam suchnaayein License ko sanshodhit karne ke roop mein vyakhyaat nahi ki ja sakti hain.

Aap apne sanshodhanon mein apna alag copyright vivaran jod sakte hain aur apne sanshodhanon ke upyog, punah utpadan, ya vitran ke liye, ya samagra roop se aisi kisi bhi Derivative Works ke liye atirikt ya alag license sharten aur niyam pradaan kar sakte hain, yadi Work ka aapka upyog, punah utpadan, aur vitran anyatha is License mein batai gayi sharton ka palan karta hai.

5. Yogdaanon ka Jama Karna​

Jab tak ki aap spasht roop se anyatha na bataayein, Licensor ko Work mein shaamil karne ke liye aap dwara anayachit roop se jama kiya gaya koi bhi Contribution, bina kisi atirikt sharton ya niyam ke, is License ki sharton aur niyam-o-sharton ke tahat hoga. Uprokt ke bawajood, yahaan kuch bhi kisi alag license samjhote ki sharton ko badalega ya sanshodhit karega jo aapne aise Contributions ke sambandh mein Licensor ke saath kiya ho sakta hai.

6. Trademarks​

Yeh License Licensor ke vyaparik naam, trademark, service mark, ya product naam ka upyog karne ki anumati nahi deti hai, sivay Work ki utpatti ka varnan karne aur NOTICE file ki samagri ko punah utpadit karne ke liye aavashyak aur rivaajee upyog ke liye.

7. Warranty ka Sankshiptikaran​

Jab tak kee laagu kaanoon dwara aavashyak na ho ya likhit roop mein sahamat na kiya gaya ho, Licensor Work (aur pratyek Contributor apanee Contributions) ko "AS IS" BASIS, WITHOUT WARRANTIES OR CONDITIONS OF ANY KIND par pradaan karata hai, chaahe vyakt kiya gaya ho ya nihit ho, isamen seemit na hote hue, TITLE, NON-INFRINGEMENT, MERCHANTABILITY, ya FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE kee koi bhee vaarantiyaan ya sharten shaamil hain. Work ka upayog ya punarvitaran karane kee upayuktata nirdhaarit karane ke lie aap pooree tarah se jimmedaar hain aur is License ke tahat anumatiyon ke aapake abhyaas se judaa sabhee jokhimon ko grahan karate hain.

8. Daayitva kee Seema​

Kisee bhee sthiti mein aur kisee bhee kaanoonee siddhaant ke tahat, chaahe tort (laaparavahee shaamil hai), anusaar, ya anyatha, jab tak kee laagu kaanoon dwara aavashyak na ho (jaise ki soochee-samajhkar aur ghor laaparavahee ke kaary) ya likhit roop mein sahamat na kiya gaya ho, to koee bhee Contributor aapako nukasaan ke lie jimmedaar nahin hoga, jisamen seedha, aprakat, vishesh, aakasmik, ya parinaamee nukasaan shaamil hain jo is License ke parinaamasvaroop ya Work ke upayog ya upayog karane mein asamarthata se utpann hote hain (jisamen achchhee-ichchha kee haani, kaam rukana, computer mein kharaabee ya gadabadee, ya koee bhee aur sabhee anya vaanijyik nukasaan ya haani shaamil hain, lekin seemit nahin hain), bhale hee aise Contributor ko aise nukasaan kee sambhaavana ke baare mein salaah dee gaee ho.

9. Vaaranti ya Atirikt Daayitva Sveekaarana​

Work ya usake Derivative Works ka punarvitaran karate samay, aap samarthan, vaaranti, kshatipoorti, ya anya daayitva badhyataon aur/ya adhikaron kee sveekruti ke lie peshakash karane ka, aur shulk lene ka, chunav kar sakate hain jo is License ke anuroop hain. Haalaanki, aisee badhyataon ko sveekaar karate samay, aap keval apane svayan kee or se aur apanee ekamatr jimmedaaree par kaam kar sakate hain, na ki kisee anya Contributor kee or se, aur keval tabhee jab aap kisee bhee vaaranti ya atirikt daayitva ko sveekaar karane ke kaaran, aise Contributor ko hone vaale kisee bhee daayitva ke lie, ya aise Contributor par kiye gae daavon ke lie, kshatipoorti, vishvaas, aur Contributor ko surakshit rakhane ke lie sahamat hote hain.
